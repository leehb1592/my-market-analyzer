Western Forest Products Inc. (WFP) is a Canada-based softwood forest products company  operating in the coastal region of British Columbia. The Company's primary business includes timber harvesting  reforestation  forest management  sawmilling logs into lumber  wood chips and lumber remanufacturing. WFP's lumber products are currently sold in over 25 countries worldwide. The principal wholly owned operating subsidiaries of the Company are Western Lumber Sales Limited (which sells into the United States)  Western Forest Products Japan Ltd. (which sells into Japan)  and WFP Quatsino Navigation Limited (the beneficial owner of a number of the Company's non-core assets).,Read More
