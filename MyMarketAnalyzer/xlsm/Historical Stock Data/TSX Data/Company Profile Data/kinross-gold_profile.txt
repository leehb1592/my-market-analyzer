Kinross Gold Corporation is a senior gold mining company. The Company is engaged in gold mining and related activities  including exploration and acquisition of gold-bearing properties  the extraction and processing of gold-containing ore  and reclamation of gold mining properties. The Company's segments include Fort Knox  Round Mountain  Kettle River-Buckhorn  Kupol  Paracatu  Maricunga  Tasiast and Chirano. Its gold production and exploration activities are carried out principally in Canada  the United States  the Russian Federation  Brazil  Chile  Ghana and Mauritania.
