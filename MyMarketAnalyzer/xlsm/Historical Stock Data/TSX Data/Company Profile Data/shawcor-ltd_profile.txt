ShawCor Ltd. is an energy services company. The Company operates through two business segment Pipeline and Pipe Services  and Petrochemical and Industrial. The Pipeline and Pipe Services segment includes the Bredero Shaw  CanusaCPS  Shaw Pipeline Services  Flexpipe Systems  Guardian  Socotherm and Desert NDT divisions. The Petrochemical and Industrial segment includes the DSG-Canusa and ShawFlex divisions.
