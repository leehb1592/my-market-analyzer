Canfor Corp is a Canada-based integrated forest products company with interests in British Columbia  Alberta  North and South Carolina  Alabama  Georgia and Mississippi. The Company produces primarily softwood lumber and specialized wood products. Furthermore  It controls Canfor Pulp Products Inc  a producer of market northern bleached softwood kraft pulp  high performance kraft paper  as well as bleached chemi-thermo mechanical pulp.
