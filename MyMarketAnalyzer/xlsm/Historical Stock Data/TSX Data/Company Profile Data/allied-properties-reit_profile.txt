Allied Properties Real Estate Investment Trust (Allied) is a Canada-based closed-end real estate investment trust (REIT). The Company is an owner  manager and developer of urban office properties. Allied has interests in various properties in Kitchener  Ontario; Toronto  Ontario  and Calgary  Alberta. The Trust operates in three geographical locations which include Eastern Canada (Montreal  Quebec City and Ottawa)  Central Canada (Toronto and Kitchener) and Western Canada (Winnipeg  Calgary  Edmonton  Vancouver and Victoria). Its subsidiaries include Allied Properties Management Trust  Allied Properties Management Limited Partnership and Allied Properties Management GP Limited.
