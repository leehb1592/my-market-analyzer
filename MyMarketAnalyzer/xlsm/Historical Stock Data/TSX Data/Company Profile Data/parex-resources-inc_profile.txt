Parex Resources  Inc. is a Canada-based oil and gas company. The Company is engaged in the exploration  development  production and marketing of oil and natural gas in South America. The Company  through its subsidiaries  holds interests in onshore exploration and production blocks totaling approximately 2 616 897 gross acres. The Company's principal operating subsidiaries include Parex Resources Colombia  Ltd.  Ramshorn International Limited  Verano Energy Limited S.C. and Parex Resources (Trinidad)  Ltd.
